AIR JORDAN


Since the very first reveal in 1985, the media, the business community, and the general public have all been anticipating new releases. AIR JORDANs dominate the market in terms of sales and demand, setting higher standards in design and innovation with each new release. The current generation of AIR JORDANS builds on the long heritage of the brand's unique designs with varying colorways. The franchise continues to lead and reshape the footwear market into the future inspired by Michael Jordan.
